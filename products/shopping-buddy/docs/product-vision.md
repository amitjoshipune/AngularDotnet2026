# Product Vision

## Product Name

**BookMyShoppingBuddy**

*(Portfolio demo — also documented as Temporary Social Activity Partner Platform)*

---

# Mission

Help women in Indian cities — especially senior citizens, single professionals, and busy mothers —
find a **verified same-gender shopping companion** for short trips to **public malls and markets**.

Initial pilot geography: **Pune metro** (Wakad, Aundh, Hadapsar, Chinchwad, Hinjawadi, Kharadi, and nearby).

---

# Primary use cases

* Mall shopping with bag-carrying help and billing queues
* Local market visits (saree, vegetables, festival shopping)
* Grocery and pharmacy runs at supermarkets
* Companionship for women who prefer not to shop alone

---

# Non Goals

The platform shall NOT support:

* Dating or matchmaking
* Escort or romantic services
* Cross-gender pairing in v1 (women ↔ women only)
* Home visits or hotel meetings
* Private residence meetings
* Overnight stays
* Alcohol-focused outings
* Transportation or courier services
* Domestic work or medical services
* Phone number or social media exchange outside the app

---

# User Personas

* Senior women shopping alone
* Single ladies new to a Pune locality
* Married women with children who need convenience, not judgment
* Verified women buddies (retirees, homemakers, part-time professionals)

---

# Trust & verification

* Mobile OTP
* Aadhaar e-KYC + selfie match
* Optional police verification badge for buddies
* Emergency contact alerts
* Optional live location sharing with family
* In-app chat and reporting only

---

# Initial Target Market

Pune → Maharashtra → Tier-1 India

---

# Success Metrics

* 1000 verified users
* 500 completed shopping trips
* Average rating above 4.5
* Less than 1% abuse reports
