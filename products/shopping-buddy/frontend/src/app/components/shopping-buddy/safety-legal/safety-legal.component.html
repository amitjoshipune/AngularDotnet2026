Safety, legal & trust

BookMyShoppingBuddy is designed to reduce social, moral, and legal risk — not create it. This page summarizes the product intent. It is not legal advice; consult a qualified Indian advocate before commercial launch.

Platform position: We are an intermediary connecting verified women for short, public shopping trips. We do not offer dating, transport, domestic work, escort services, or private meetings.
